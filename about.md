# About this site

MP Record is a signposting service. For every current Member of Parliament, it collects direct links to their official public records — voting history, registered financial interests, declared donations, expenses, and speeches — so you can read them at the source.

## What we publish

Links. Nothing else. Every card on an MP's page opens a record published by Parliament, a statutory regulator, or a government body. This site adds no commentary, no summaries, and no user-submitted content, and it does not accept contributions.

## Where the data comes from

The list of MPs, their parties, and constituencies is generated automatically from the official [UK Parliament Members API](https://members-api.parliament.uk/), and regenerated regularly so it stays current after by-elections and general elections.

## A note on name-based searches

Some databases (Electoral Commission donations, Companies House) are searched by name rather than by a unique identifier, so results can include other people who share an MP's name. Always confirm details in the record itself before drawing conclusions.
